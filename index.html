Compound Interest Formula

Adjust the principal, annual rate, and number of periods. The equation, chart, and table update automatically. The rate is entered as a percentage (e.g. 5 for 5%); it is divided by 100 in the formula.

Enter principal (digits only; commas optional), annual rate as a percentage, and number of periods (whole number). Values must stay within the allowed ranges. Monetary amounts are shown as USD prefixed to the number with no space (for example USD1,234.56); screen readers use the word dollars for those values.
